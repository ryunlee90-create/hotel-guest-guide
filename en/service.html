Guest Service

Guest Services

Please check the basic services available during your stay.

Luggage storage
Luggage storage is available before check-in and after check-out.
Rental items
Some items such as wine openers, chargers, and converters may be available for rental.
Extra towels
If you need extra towels, please contact the Front Desk.
Water
Water purifiers are available on each floor of Building A.
Guests staying in Building B may use the water purifier near the banquet hall on the lobby floor.
Additional bottled water can be purchased at the convenience store in the hotel.
Microwave
A microwave is available at the CU convenience store next to the Front Desk.
Food delivery
Please receive food delivery directly in the lobby in front of the Front Desk.
Rental items are limited in quantity. Please return them to the Front Desk after use.
