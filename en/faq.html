Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Please check the frequently requested information below. For further assistance, contact the Front Desk.

Stay

Stay Information

What time are check-in and check-out?

Check-in is available from 3:00 PM, and check-out is until 11:00 AM.

Can I extend my check-out time?

Late check-out is subject to room availability on the day. It may be available until 2:00 PM at the latest, with an additional charge of KRW 11,000 per hour. Please contact the Front Desk for assistance.

How can I use Wi-Fi?

Please select the Wi-Fi network with your room number. No separate password is required.

Do I need to register my parking?

Separate parking registration is not required. Please provide your vehicle number to the Front Desk upon check-in.

Room Guide

Room Guide

How do I set the air conditioner?

Please set the operation mode on the remote control screen to “Cooling.” If the air conditioner does not work properly after setting it, please contact the Front Desk.

Where can I find the TV instructions?

Please refer to the instructions on the back of the TV remote control.

What should I do if I hear a door alert sound?

If you hear a door alert sound, please push the room door firmly until it is fully closed.

Dining

Breakfast

What are the breakfast hours and location?

Breakfast is available from 7:00 AM to 10:00 AM. Last entry is at 9:30 AM. Breakfast is served on the 1st floor of Building A.

How much is breakfast?

Breakfast is available at an advance discount rate when purchased by the previous day.

Adult advance purchase: KRW 15,000
Adult same-day purchase: KRW 17,600
Child: KRW 12,100

Breakfast vouchers purchased at the advance discount rate are non-refundable.
Until when can I purchase a breakfast voucher?

Advance discount breakfast vouchers can be purchased until 11:30 PM on the previous day. Same-day breakfast vouchers can be purchased until 9:20 AM on the day of use.

Service

Guest Services

Can I store my luggage?

Luggage storage is available before check-in and after check-out.

What items are available for rental?

Some items such as wine openers, chargers, and converters may be available for rental. Rental items are limited in quantity. Please return them to the Front Desk after use.

Can I request extra towels?

If you need extra towels, please contact the Front Desk.

Where is the water purifier?

Water purifiers are available on each floor of Building A. Guests staying in Building B may use the water purifier near the banquet hall on the lobby floor.

Can I request extra bottled water?

Additional bottled water can be purchased at the convenience store in the hotel.

Where can I use a microwave?

A microwave is available at the CU convenience store next to the Front Desk.

Where can I receive food delivery?

Please receive food delivery directly in the lobby in front of the Front Desk.

Can the hotel call a taxi for me?

The Front Desk can assist with calling a Kakao Taxi. Waiting time may vary depending on taxi availability.

Facilities & Policy

Facilities & Room Policy

Where is the convenience store?

The CU convenience store is located next to the Front Desk. It is open 24 hours, and operates unmanned from 10:00 PM to 2:00 PM the following day. During unmanned hours, purchase of some items such as cigarettes and alcoholic beverages may be restricted.

Is smoking allowed in the room?

All rooms are non-smoking. Please use the designated smoking area. Additional charges may apply if smoking occurs inside the room.

Is cooking allowed in the room?

Cooking is not allowed inside the room. We appreciate your cooperation for a safe and comfortable stay.

How should I use the terrace screen door?

Please keep the terrace screen door closed at all times.

How is room cleaning handled?

Room cleaning is provided according to hotel operating standards. Cleaning standards and available times may vary depending on hotel operations.

How is trash collected?

Please leave trash in your room. It will be collected while you are out or after check-out.

Benefits & Lost and Found

Partner Benefits & Lost and Found

How can I use partner benefits?

Partner benefits are available during your stay. Please visit the Front Desk to receive a benefit voucher before use.

How can I check for lost items?

Lost items found at the hotel are stored for up to 30 days. Please contact or visit the Front Desk for assistance with lost items.

Can I get information about nearby restaurants or attractions?

A nearby restaurant list and local area map are available at the Front Desk.
